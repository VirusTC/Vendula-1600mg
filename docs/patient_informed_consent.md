# Patient Informed Consent Form (ICF)
**Study Code:** VVP-PREP-001  
**Product Reference:** Oral Botanical Volatile Prophylaxis (PrEP)  

## Introduction & Voluntariness
You are being asked to participate in a clinical research study evaluating an oral botanical pill designed to prevent mosquito bites from the inside out. Your participation is completely voluntary. You may withdraw from this study at any time without penalty or loss of medical benefits.

## How It Works
If you agree to participate, you will take one oral dose of the botanical compound daily. The ingredient works by dissolving into your body’s natural lipid carriers and exiting continuously through your sweat glands. This creates an invisible barrier around your skin that blocks mosquitoes from smelling you. Because it comes from your pores, it **cannot wash off, rub off, or sweat off.** 

If a mosquito manages to bite you, the compound is already present in your bloodstream and skin tissue to immediately fight the swelling, cellular tissue damage, and inflammation caused by the mosquito’s saliva.

## What is Required of You
1. Take the medication daily with a meal as prescribed.
2. Attend weekly follow-up visits to check your skin and health parameters.
3. Allow the study team to perform brief skin-swab testing to measure the amount of the compound exiting your pores.
4. Report any mosquito bites, skin redness, or unusual symptoms to the clinic immediately.
